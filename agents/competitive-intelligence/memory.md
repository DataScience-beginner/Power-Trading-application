# Competitive Intelligence Memory

## 2026-07-11 baseline

Ten platforms were benchmarked: GridBeyond, Fluence Mosaic, Stem PowerTrack Optimizer, Kraken, Gridmatic, Pexapark, Bidgely UtilityAI, Siemens Gridscale X, Schneider EcoStruxure Resource Advisor, and EnergyCAP.

Stable market patterns:

- Domain forecasting and optimization create the decision; a chatbot explains and orchestrates it.
- Leading systems combine internal operational data with external weather, market, tariff, and grid signals.
- Probabilistic forecasts and explicit constraints are safer than single-number predictions.
- Valuable automation is controlled, auditable, and frequently human-in-the-loop.
- A governed energy data model is the foundation for forecasting, optimization, and agentic workflows.

Innowatt's intended differentiation is Indian C&I procurement optimization across Solar, IEX, and TNEB with 15-minute schedules, solar-expiry awareness, explainable recommendations, and multi-tenant controls.

Revalidate vendor capabilities before major product decisions because product names and features change.
