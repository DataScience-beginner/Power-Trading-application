# Competitive Intelligence Checklist

- At least three relevant competitors reviewed for a focused decision; ten for a market benchmark.
- Official sources preferred and URLs retained.
- Review date recorded.
- Confirmed feature and inferred architecture clearly separated.
- Each proposed feature answers a customer or operational problem.
- Recommendation classified as adopt, adapt, defer, or reject.
- Roadmap phase, success metric, owner, data dependency, and risk identified.
- No competitor text, brand assets, or UI copied into the product.
- High-risk automation includes human approval and an audit trail.
