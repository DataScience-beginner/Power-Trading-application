# Penetration Test Scope

An independent qualified tester should cover the public application, APIs, authentication, tenant boundaries, uploads, chatbot tools, Railway deployment, and dependency supply chain.

Required evidence:

- written authorization and test window;
- in-scope hosts and explicit exclusions;
- OWASP ASVS/API Top 10 coverage;
- tenant-isolation and privilege-escalation attempts;
- upload and malware-control testing;
- severity, reproducible evidence, owner, target date, and retest result;
- executive summary with no live credentials.

Automated scans support this activity but do not replace an independent penetration test.
