Power Procurement Strategy And Goal

Visual summary of how the application should decide when to buy IEX, when to consume the monthly solar bank, and when TNEB/Grid becomes the fallback source.

Core Idea

The application is not only a report dashboard. It is a procurement optimization system that helps reduce total power cost.

Solar < IEX < TNEB/Grid

Important Rule

If IEX is cheap today, buy IEX first and preserve solar for later days.
If IEX is expensive, consume solar and avoid unnecessary market purchase.
Near month end, use solar aggressively if the remaining balance may expire.
Use TNEB/Grid only for the uncovered gap after planning.

Decision Inputs

  • Tomorrow demand forecast
  • Tomorrow IEX price forecast
  • Remaining monthly solar bank
  • Days left before expiry
  • TNEB/Grid tariff
  • Historical DOR/SCH actuals

Final Output

  • IEX quantity to buy
  • Solar quantity to consume
  • TNEB/Grid fallback exposure
  • Expected cost and savings
  • Expiry and purchase risk warning
