Client And Data Model Architecture

Separate architecture view for the client hierarchy, current report/database model, and proposed planning layer for solar banking, demand forecasting, IEX forecasting, and procurement recommendations.

Architecture Summary

Client
Top-level business entity. A client can have many portfolios and one monthly solar bank.
Current model
Stores actual uploaded DOR/SCH files, time-slot transactions, monthly calculations, and energy schedule metrics.
Solar bank
Proposed client/month layer because solar is common to the client and expires at month end.
Planning layer
Proposed tables for demand forecast, IEX price forecast, procurement plan, and 96-slot recommendation detail.

Current Tables

  • clients
  • portfolios
  • daily_files
  • transactions
  • monthly_calculations
  • energy_schedule_months
  • energy_schedule_days

Proposed Tables

  • solar_banks
  • solar_bank_entries
  • demand_forecasts
  • iex_price_forecasts
  • procurement_plans
  • procurement_plan_timeslots

Design Principle

Actual tables answer what happened. Planning tables answer what should we do tomorrow.
